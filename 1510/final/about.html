About Me

Hello there! Welcome to my page. If you've stumbled on this page, it means you are probably interested in composting! Composting is a great way to reduce your carbon footprint, and help the environment.

I first found a passion for the environment in college. I attended the University of Georgia from 2015-2020, and I was an Ecology major. I quickly found this to be something I am incredibly interested in, and found my niche with composting and reducing waste. I figured, instead of filling landfills with more waste, why not reuse it for better purposes?

So, here we are. I am still learning, as are you! Together, we can learn all about composting and make this planet a better place!
