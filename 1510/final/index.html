Composting... What is it?

Composting is the process of breaking down, or "cooking", organic matter into a substance that is rich in plant nutrients and organisms.

Benefits of Composting Include:

  • Add nutrients to your soil
  • Introduce valuable organisms to your soil /Mulch
  • Recycles kitchen and yard waste
  • Reduces landfill waste
  • Good for the environment
